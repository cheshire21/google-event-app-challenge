# Product Mission

## Problem

Users who manage appointments or meetings need a single place to create bookings without worrying about double-booking themselves — both against other in-app bookings and against events already on their personal Google Calendar.

## Target Users

Individuals and small teams who take appointments or schedule meetings and want a lightweight, Google-integrated booking tool that prevents scheduling conflicts automatically.

## Solution

Nook is a warm, friendly booking app that syncs read-only with the user's Google Calendar to perform real-time conflict detection. When creating or rescheduling a booking, the app checks against both existing in-app bookings and Google Calendar events, blocking the action on conflict and confirming immediately when the slot is free.
